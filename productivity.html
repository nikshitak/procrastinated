Ideal Plan

1. Set a timer for 25 minutes, and actually work during that time (if you're working, the time will go a lot faster than you think

2. After the 25 minutes, take a short 5 minute break

3. This 5 minute break is for you to get up, stretch, get a glass of water, just get away from your desk

4. Repeat 3 more times (another 25 minute work session + 5 minutes break).

5. Take a longer break (15 minutes), you've earned it!
